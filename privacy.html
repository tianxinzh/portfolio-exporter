Privacy Policy — Portfolio Exporter

Last updated: 2026-06-01

Portfolio Exporter runs entirely in your browser.

  • What it accesses: while you are on robinhood.com, it reads the holdings data your browser already receives from Robinhood (equities, options, crypto, and their prices) to convert it into a file you download.
  • What it stores: only your export preferences (format, columns, filters, account label) via Chrome's storage API. Nothing else.
  • What it sends: nothing. No servers, no analytics, no trackers, no remote code. Your holdings never leave your computer except as the file you choose to download.
  • Permissions: storage (remember settings), downloads (save your export), access to robinhood.com (read holdings on the page).

Not affiliated with or endorsed by Robinhood.
